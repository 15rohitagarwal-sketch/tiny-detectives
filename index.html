Meet the Founder

A School Born from Love & Purpose

Tiny Detectives was founded by Neeru Agarwal with a deeply personal belief: every child deserves to be truly known and personally nurtured during their most important, formative years.

With years of experience in early childhood education, Neeru created a school that feels less like an institution and more like an extended family — where children are safe to explore, ask questions, make mistakes, and grow at their own pace.

The name Tiny Detectives captures our philosophy: little ones are natural investigators, and our job is to nurture that curiosity with love, patience, and exceptional teaching.

🌱 Our Vision

Every child leaves confident, curious, and ready for the world

💡 Our Philosophy

Learning through play, discovery & genuine relationships

❤️ Our Promise

Your child will be known by name, personality & potential

🤝 Our Commitment

Open, honest communication with parents — always
